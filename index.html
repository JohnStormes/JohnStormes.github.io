Problem statement: Attendance Subscriptions

Students must pay for multiple subscription services in order to have their attendance counted in classes.

Affinity diagram: Attendance Subscriptions

our affinity diagram is a brainstorm board for ways to improve the system of attendance subscriptions at universities. Firstly, an application needs to be made to account for large amounts of students. Secondly, the university needs to be consulted and convinced to apply the program. Thirdly, the program should expand beyond a single classroom or school. Also, attendance needs to be accounted for accurately without the possibility of absent students clocking in. Finally, pricing and cost need to be considered.

Team sketches: Attendance Subscriptions

Our team sketches represent different visual concepts for our attendance application. The idea is that any given student can sign into their school's database, see their schedule based on the date and semester, and then sign into their class in person with a interactive sign in module. My sketch displays the log in screen, then the course selection screen, then the sign in screen.
